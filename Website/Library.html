Using the Library

Accessing the Library
The Library is Open 24/7

To enter the Library and borrow library materials you will need a campus card.

To use IT facilities you will need an IT Account.

New Students

For new students acquiring a campus card and IT account is part of the online registration process and you will be given your campus card and IT account details at the time of registration after arriving at UEA.

Find everything you need to know about the library on our Start your journey pages.

New Staff

Staff paid by and working at UEA should ensure that their signed contract of employment has been returned to UEA Human Resources. When you start working at UEA, please visit the IT Service Desk to get your campus card which will allow entry into the Library.

Campus Cards

Further information can be found about IT Accounts and Campus Cards from IT Services.

Welcome

UEA Library is open 24/7 for essential use and individual study only

It is a legal requirement to wear a face covering in the Library at all times unless you are exempt. You will need a working campus card to get in the Library. We are sorry but we cannot currently accept visitors.
